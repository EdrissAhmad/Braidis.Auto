What We Do

Our Services

From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we've got you covered year-round.

General Repairs

Brakes, exhaust, steering, suspension — we diagnose and fix issues correctly the first time, at prices that won't surprise you.

Tire Services

Winter and all-season tire changeovers, balancing, and inspections. Stay safe on the road through every season.

Oil & Fluid Changes

Regular maintenance keeps your engine healthy. Quick, affordable oil changes and full fluid inspections on every visit.

Diagnostics

Check engine light on? We use modern diagnostic tools to pinpoint the problem fast, so you know exactly what needs attention.

Brakes & Safety

Brake pads, rotors, and callipers inspected and replaced. We never cut corners when it comes to your safety.

Winter Prep

Get your vehicle winter-ready with battery checks, antifreeze top-ups, winter tire installs, and full seasonal inspections.

Air & Cabin Filter Replacement

Clean filters improve engine performance and air quality inside your cabin. Quick swap, big difference — we check both on every visit.

Air Conditioning

A/C not blowing cold? We recharge, inspect, and repair your vehicle's air conditioning system so you stay comfortable year-round.

Tire Rotation

Regular tire rotation extends tire life and improves handling. We rotate and re-torque to manufacturer specs every time.

Electrical Repairs

From dead batteries to faulty wiring, sensors, and charging systems — we diagnose and fix electrical issues accurately and efficiently.

Exhaust Repair

Loud rumble, rust, or failed emissions? We inspect, repair, and replace exhaust components to keep your vehicle quiet and compliant.

Suspension Repair

Rough ride, pulling, or uneven wear? We inspect and replace shocks, struts, control arms, and bushings to restore a smooth, safe ride.

Transmission Service

Slipping gears or delayed shifts? We service both automatic and manual transmissions — fluid changes, diagnostics, and full repairs.

Rust Proofing

Protect your vehicle from Ontario winters. We apply professional rust proofing treatments to extend your vehicle's life and resale value.
